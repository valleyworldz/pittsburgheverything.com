# Best Restaurants in Pittsburgh Right Now (By Neighborhood)

Pittsburgh's food scene has exploded in recent years, offering everything from classic pierogi and Primanti Bros sandwiches to modern farm-to-table cuisine and international flavors. Here's our current guide to the best restaurants, organized by neighborhood for easy exploration.

---

## Quick Picks

- **Best overall:** The Porch at Schenley - modern American with views
- **Best classic Pittsburgh:** Primanti Bros - the sandwich that built the city
- **Best pizza:** Fat Head's Saloon - craft beer and artisanal pies
- **Best brunch:** Walnut Grill - creative takes on breakfast classics
- **Best hidden gem:** Legume - vegetable-forward fine dining
